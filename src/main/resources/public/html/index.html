Play Smarter, Play harder, Play Genius

We want you to have score more goals, make more passes, beat more defenders. We want you to have more fun.

The Play Genius will help you do that. Its a small wearable device that tells you and your team mates who sprinted the quickest, which parts of the pitch you were most involved in, and who ran the most

If you want to see it in action then lets play!
